Avocado Sandwich

Description

A lunchtime favorite that is simple, delicious, and refreshing! Particularly scrumptious on toasted whole grain bread. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices provolone cheese
  • 1/4 avocado - peeled, pitted, and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tomato, diced
  • Mayonnaise to spread on bread

Steps

  1. Spread mayonnaise on bread and lay on pan
  2. Place cheese on bread and top with avocado and tomato slices, respectively. Season tomato and avocado with salt and pepper
  3. Place second slice of bread on top
  4. Sear until bread is golden then flip
  5. Repeat and enjoy!
