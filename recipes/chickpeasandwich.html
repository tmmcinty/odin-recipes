Chickpea Sandwich

Description

Serve this tasty vegetarian sandwich on crusty whole grain bread with hummus and spinach. Complete with green onions and celery. Season with salt, pepper and additional seasonings of choice for best results. Your favorite salad dressing can be substituted for the mayo.

Ingredients

  • 1 can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped
  • 3 sprigs green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • whole grain bread
  • hummus to spread on bread
  • spinach to top on bread

Steps

  1. Drain and rinse chicpeas. Pour chickpeas into a medium size mixing bowl and mash witha fork.
  2. Mix in celery, green onion, maynonaise (to taste), dill, salt and pepper.
  3. On toasted bread, spread with hummus and top with spinach.
  4. Scoop chickpea filling onto toast and enjoy!
